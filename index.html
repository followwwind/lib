Story summary

In 2030, Dr. Lijah discovered that his son Adam suddenly became a panda. Soon afterwards, many humans around the world also became pandas with special abilities due to genetic mutations caused by staying up late. The incident triggered global panic and the government began to control it. Everyone wanted to send the pandas to the Life Evolution Laboratory for experimentation. This led the pandas and panda supporters to establish an organization called Sleeplesspanda. With Dr. Lijah as the leader of the organization, the Sleeplesspanda organization confronted the panda protesters. After investigation, Dr. Lijah found that the mutation event was caused by a panda from the future. The panda from the future came to the present through time and space to change the past and avoid the war between pandas and humans caused by the future world. Therefore, he stayed in Antarctica. A huge spaceship wants to let the Pandaren leave the earth life temporarily in the spaceship.

The governments of various countries began to arrest the pandamen. The doctors gathered the pandas from around the world to gather in the Life Development Laboratory. Adam drove the spacecraft to rescue all the pandamen, and then flew to Planet "Gliese 667"
